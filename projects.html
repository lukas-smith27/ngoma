WOMEN & CHILDREN

Ngoma partners with the The Crew and Campus Life, in Noble County, to help support vital programs and activities for Northeast Indiana youth.

Ngoma partners with East Noble Schools to provide support and activities for youth and encouragement to teachers and staff throughout the school year.
