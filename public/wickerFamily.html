THE WICKER FAMILY

Ngoma Center, Inc. was established in 2017 and serves as a nonprofit Christian humanitarian relief organization dedicated to help alleviate the suffering of children and their families throughout northeast Indiana and around the world. Ngoma's mission is to increase self sufficiency by providing food, clothing, training and medical care without regard to political affiliation, religious belief, or ethnic identity. At Ngoma we believe God has called us to meet the emotional, spiritual and physical needs of those around us with Christ based principles. God has placed a vision on our hearts to "build a foundation for the future" 1 Timothy 6:19.
