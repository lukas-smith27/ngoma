Ngoma is a nonprofit Christian organization dedicated to help alleviate the suffering of children and their families throughout Northeast Indiana and around the world.


Ngoma's mission is to increase self sufficiency by providing food, clothing, training and medical care without regard to political affiliation, religious belief, or ethnic identity.
