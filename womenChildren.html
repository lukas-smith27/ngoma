WOMEN & CHILDREN

Children are the heart of Ngoma Center Inc. Our board member have a heart for helping struggling families in Northeast Indiana. We partner with local organizations, such as The Noble House and Life & Family Services to provide hope to women and children that find themselves homeless, struggle with addiction or are victims of domestic violence.
